Today's Activity

In the spirit of using digital resources for personalized learning, today you are going to use the informaiton in this section to explore and then share what you learned in a Google document or lesson plan.

  1. Explore:~20 minutes

    Use the resources in the buttons below to discover new information about Google Drive and file collaboration "in the cloud".

  2. Create:~20 minutes

    Working in your CMS Google Account, create a resource from Google Drive (Doc, Sheet, Slide, Form, or Drawing). Make it something relevant for you as an educator or for your students as learners.

  3. Share:~5 minutes

    4. Click on the red button to access the shared folder where you can experiment with docs, sheets, and slides.
